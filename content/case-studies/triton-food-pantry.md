## Overview

One in four UCSD students experiences food insecurity, yet the campus food pantry is chronically underutilized. I led a service design project to understand why — and fix it.

## Research

Through 14 interviews with current and lapsed pantry users, I uncovered the central barrier: **social stigma**. Students feared being seen picking up food assistance.

## Insights

- The physical queue was visible from a busy walkway
- The sign-up form asked for financial proof in a public kiosk
- No digital option existed to schedule pickups discreetly

## Design Interventions

1. A private online scheduling system with discreet confirmation emails
2. A redesigned pickup flow with a covered entrance
3. Rebrand language from "pantry" to "student resource hub"

## Impact

After implementation, monthly users increased by 41% within one semester. The digital scheduling system was adopted by 3 other UC campuses.
