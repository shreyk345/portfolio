## Overview

An immersive virtual workspace designed for solo creatives — a place to focus, create, and feel less alone while working remotely.

## Concept

During a period of remote study, I found myself missing the ambient energy of shared studios. This project asked: can a virtual space replicate the feeling of working alongside other people?

## Process

Built entirely in Unreal Engine with custom assets modelled in Blender. The environment includes:

- Ambient sound design (soft studio noise, distant music)
- Dynamic lighting that follows a simulated day/night cycle
- Interactive elements: a working easel, bookshelf, window with weather

## What I Learned

Presence in VR is more about peripheral detail than fidelity. Small things — a plant swaying, distant typing sounds — contributed more to immersion than high-polygon models.

## Next Steps

Exploring multi-user sessions so artists can share the space while working on their own projects in parallel.
